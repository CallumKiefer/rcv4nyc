Why change our voting system?

The voting system we use in NY and much of the US is call "first past the post." This is the way of counting votes you're likely most familiar with, but it has many issues and often forces voters to chose between the lesser of two evils.

We imagine a world where every voter could vote for a candidate that truly represents them.

Check out FairVote's solutions for more information on alternative systems.
